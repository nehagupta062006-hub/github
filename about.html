Our story

Small things, held onto.

We started miniprint because we noticed something quiet and strange: the more photos we took, the fewer we actually saw.

Ten-thousand images. All locked inside a screen. Somewhere along the way, memory stopped being tangible. So we designed a tiny printer — one that fits in a pocket, needs no ink, and turns any phone photo into something you can pin to a wall, tape into a journal, or slip inside a friend's card.

miniprint isn't about nostalgia. It's about noticing. About making room for the small, worth-keeping moments in an increasingly weightless world.

Designed in Copenhagen. Made to travel everywhere.

A small team obsessed with tactile things. We test every printer by hand before it leaves us.
